How it works

From brief to
showtime.

One point of contact from initial call to the last light going out. No handoffs, no confusion.

01
Brief call
Tell me the event — capacity, venue, vibe, budget, date. 15 mins is enough.
02
Spec & quote
I put together a production spec and quote tailored to what you actually need.
03
Venue visit
Site check — acoustics, power, rigging points, sight lines. I plan everything in advance.
04
Load in & set up
I arrive early. Everything is installed, tested, and soundchecked before doors open.
05

I'm on the desk — or behind the camera — for the whole event. Problems get solved before they become problems.
